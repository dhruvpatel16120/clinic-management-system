# First 5 Clinics Outreach Playbook

Use this before you start calling clinics in Saharanpur and nearby areas.

## Goal

Do not try to close a long contract on the first call. The first goal is:

1. confirm the clinic is a fit
2. book a 10-15 minute demo
3. move the owner or front-desk lead into a 14-day pilot

## Best Fit for the First 5 Calls

Start with clinics that look like this:

- single-doctor or small multi-doctor OPD clinics
- one owner plus 1-3 front-desk staff
- visible patient queue or busy waiting area
- still using registers, WhatsApp, Excel, or manual bills
- owner is reachable directly or through a receptionist

Avoid starting with:

- hospitals
- telemedicine businesses
- clinics asking for custom enterprise integrations on day one
- owners who want only a free software trial with no onboarding

## Offer to Say Verbatim

Use one clean offer:

> We set up a 14-day ClinicNova pilot for your clinic, onboard the owner and front desk, and show appointments, queue, prescriptions, and billing in one workflow. If your team uses it daily and it fits, we move you to a monthly plan.

## 30-Second Phone Opener

Use this when you reach the owner or clinic manager:

> Hi, I am reaching out because we are rolling out ClinicNova for local clinics. It helps the front desk and doctor manage appointments, patient queue, prescriptions, and billing in one system instead of registers, WhatsApp, and manual follow-up. I wanted to see if you would be open to a short 10-minute demo this week.

## If Receptionist Answers First

Use this:

> Hi, I am calling about a clinic workflow system that helps with appointments, token queue, prescriptions, and billing. I only need 30 seconds to understand if this is relevant for the doctor or clinic owner. Who usually handles software or operations decisions there?

If they ask for WhatsApp first:

> Absolutely. I will send a short note and request a 10-minute demo slot. What is the best number and name to mention?

## Discovery Questions

Do not ask all of these. Pick 3-4 based on the conversation.

- How do you currently manage appointments and walk-in patients?
- Who handles the waiting queue and token flow at the clinic?
- Are prescriptions and billing still partly manual?
- How many staff members touch patient flow in a normal day?
- Do you want one system for reception and doctor workflow, or are you only looking for billing right now?
- If a 14-day pilot works, who would approve the subscription?

## How to Close the Call Into a Demo

Use this:

> This looks like the kind of workflow we are solving. I can show you the full receptionist-to-doctor flow in 10 minutes. Is tomorrow better, or the day after?

Do not end with:

- "Let me know if interested"
- "I will send details and you can review"
- "Please think and call back"

Always ask for a time.

## WhatsApp Follow-Up Template

Use this right after the call.

### English

> Hi Dr. {{name}}, this is {{your name}} from ClinicNova. As discussed, we help clinics manage appointments, token queue, prescriptions, and billing in one workflow. I would like to show you a short 10-minute demo and, if it fits, set up a 14-day pilot for your clinic. Are you available on {{day}} at {{time}}?

### Hinglish

> Hi Dr. {{name}}, main {{your name}} bol raha hoon ClinicNova se. Hum clinics ko appointments, token queue, prescriptions aur billing ek hi workflow me manage karne me help karte hain. Main aapko 10-minute ka short demo dikhana chahta hoon aur agar fit lage to 14-day pilot setup kar sakte hain. {{day}} ko {{time}} theek rahega?

## Common Objections

### We already use something

> Understood. I am not asking you to replace anything blindly. Let me show you the front-desk to billing flow in 10 minutes, and you can decide if it is better than the gaps you still have today.

### Staff will not learn new software

> That is exactly why we start as a guided pilot. We onboard the owner and front desk first, keep the workflow narrow, and only expand if the team is comfortable.

### Send pricing first

> Happy to share pricing. Before that, the useful part is seeing whether the workflow fits your clinic style. The demo is short, and then I can recommend the right plan instead of sending generic pricing.

### Call later

> Sure. I will keep it short. What day is better for a 10-minute demo so I can block it now?

## What to Track for Each Clinic

Keep a simple sheet with these columns:

- clinic name
- doctor or owner name
- number
- specialty
- current workflow tools
- daily patient load
- pain point
- demo booked or not
- follow-up date
- owner decision maker

## Success Standard for the First 5 Calls

If you achieve this, the outreach is working:

- 5 clinics called
- 3 meaningful conversations
- 2 demos booked
- 1 pilot seriously discussed

That is enough signal to improve the pitch and keep going.
